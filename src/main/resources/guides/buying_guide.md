# Property Buying Guide

This guide provides a step-by-step process for buying a new property.

## 1. Budgeting
Determine how much you can afford, including down payment and closing costs.

## 2. Mortgages
Get pre-approved for a mortgage to understand your borrowing power.

## 3. Searching
Use the `search_properties` tool to find listings that match your criteria.

## 4. Viewing
Schedule viewings for properties that interest you.

## 5. Making an Offer
Work with your agent to make a competitive offer.

## 6. Closing
Complete the legal and financial steps to finalize the purchase.
